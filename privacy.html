Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: MARCH 2026

Dram Diary™ ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Dram Diary mobile application and website at dramdiary.com.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Your email address, display name, and username when you create an account.
  • Collection data: Information you voluntarily enter about your whiskey collection, including bottle names, purchase prices, tasting notes, ratings, and photos.
  • Usage data: Basic app activity such as which features you use, to help us improve the app.
  • Device information: Device type and operating system version for technical support purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Dram Diary app and its features
  • Sync your collection data across devices
  • Display community features such as leaderboards and achievement counts
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve and develop new features
  • Send important service notifications (not marketing emails without your consent)

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider, on servers in the United States. Supabase employs industry-standard security practices including encryption at rest and in transit. You can review Supabase's privacy practices at supabase.com/privacy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your information with third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share limited data only in the following circumstances:

  • Community features: Your display name, badge count, and bottle count may be visible to other users in leaderboards. Your individual collection details are private unless you choose to share them.
  • Service providers: We use Supabase to store and process your data on our behalf, under strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

Photos

If you choose to upload photos of your bottles, these are stored in our secure cloud storage. Photos you upload are associated with your account and are not publicly visible to other users.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data and collection information will be permanently deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your collection data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@dramdiary.com.

Children's Privacy

Dram Diary is intended for users 21 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 21. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website (dramdiary.com) does not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking. We use only essential functionality to serve the site.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:
