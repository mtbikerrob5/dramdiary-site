DASHBOARD

Your shelf at a glance.
Your collection in motion.

The dashboard surfaces everything that matters the moment you open the app. Total inventory, unique expressions, open bottles, and estimated market value — all live. A timeline chart shows how your collection has grown year over year.

  • Real-time portfolio market value vs. what you paid
  • Added & Finished bottle timeline going back years
  • Recent additions with bottle art, front and center
  • AI-curated tasting flight from your own collection
  • Infinity Bottle tracking — blend and document over time

COLLECTION

Every bottle.
Every detail.

Your collection isn't just a list — it's a record. Search, filter, and sort across every bottle you own. Each entry tracks proof, age, size, fill level, storage location, purchase price, and current market value.

  • Search and filter by distillery, type, rarity, status, and more
  • Bottle art pulled automatically from the catalog
  • Fill level slider — track how much is left in every open bottle
  • Tasting notes and personal ratings for every pour
  • Pending review badge for newly submitted bottles

ANALYTICS

Data that reveals
your palate.

Dram Diary™ doesn't just store data — it surfaces patterns. See which distilleries dominate your shelf, which bottles you've held the longest, where your money is working hardest, and which open bottles you should pour through before they peak.

  • Portfolio value: total paid, market value, total gain/loss
  • Price-per-ounce breakdown — find your best and worst value
  • Spirit type and distillery breakdown charts
  • Distillery loyalty rankings across your full collection
  • Open bottle tracker with "Drink First" alerts by days open
  • Community wishlist — see what fellow collectors are hunting

COMMUNITY

Rank up.
Stand out.

Your collector rank grows as your collection does. Earn badges, climb the leaderboard, and track your progress across 36 achievements spanning seven categories. From Newcomer to Warehouse, every milestone is earned — not bought.

  • Collector ranks: Newcomer → Enthusiast → Collector → Curator → Warehouse
  • 36 achievements across Collection, Loyalty, Hunting, Variety, Pours, Community, Prestige
  • Leaderboard — bottles and badges
  • Wishlist sharing with the community
  • Your public collector profile

WHAT'S INSIDE

Everything a serious
collector needs

From barcode scanning to AI-curated tasting flights, Dram Diary™ is the only app built from the ground up for whiskey enthusiasts who take their collection seriously.

📷

BARCODE SCANNER

Scan any bottle to instantly pull details from our 40,000+ bottle catalog. Not there? Submit it and it's added to the community database after review.

∞

INFINITY BOTTLES

Create and manage infinity bottles — blends built pour by pour from your collection over time. Track every contributing bottle and document the evolution.

✦

AI TASTING FLIGHTS

Let the app curate a 3–6 bottle tasting flight from your own collection. Pick My Flight considers what you have open, your ratings, and variety — so every session is intentional.

📈

PORTFOLIO VALUE

Track what you paid versus current market value. Top gainers, price-per-ounce rankings, ROI percentage, and total gain or loss — updated as you go.

TASTING NOTES

Log detailed notes and personal ratings for every bottle you open. Build a searchable record of your palate over years of pours.

🏆

ACHIEVEMENTS & RANKS

36 achievements across 7 categories. Your collector rank advances from Newcomer to Warehouse as your collection, tasting notes, and community contributions grow.
